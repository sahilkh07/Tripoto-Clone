What can you say about festivals in India that hasn’t already been said? The entire nation comes alive when a festival is celebrated. Irrespective of religious beliefs, all communities celebrate each festival with fervour and enthusiasm. From the colourful Holi festival to Id-Ul-Fitr and from Diwali to Christmas – all festivals should be experienced once in this land of diversity. Each state has its own festival which it celebrates in a big way. While the festive rush is insane during any celebration, it adds to the fun and festivity. If you are planning a trip to India soon, try and visit the country during a festive season. The colours, sound and lights will blow you away. Festivals in India have a charm of their own and also bring people together.
